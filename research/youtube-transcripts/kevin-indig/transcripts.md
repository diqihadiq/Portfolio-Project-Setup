## Key Themes from These Transcripts

- Three-part automation framework: (1) mine insights from forums/SERPs, (2) AI drafts at scale, (3) programmatic content enhancements for aggregators
- AI drafts should be starting points, not final products - 80% is AI, 20% is human expertise and unique angles
- AI overviews are replacing product grids in e-commerce SERPs - visual content (images, video) becoming more dominant
- Different chatbots cite different sources: ChatGPT leans on Wikipedia + Reddit, Perplexity mirrors Google, all have different optimization targets
- ChatGPT traffic grew 700% YoY (2024 to 2025) - cannot ignore AI search channels
- 80% of final clicks still go to organic results, but clicks overall are dropping dramatically
- Trust now precedes relevance in how users evaluate search results - brand recognition matters before content quality
- Content structure (headings, FAQs, key takeaways, table of contents) is increasingly important for both users and AI parsers
- SEO KPIs are shifting from clicks to leading indicators: share of voice, rankings, brand visibility, trust signals
- Some companies are now rationally choosing to block AI crawlers - publishers and affiliate sites especially
