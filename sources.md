## Why These 10 Experts?

The selection criteria:

1. **Practitioner, not just commentator** - they run campaigns, manage clients, or ship products, not just write about trends
2. **Active in 2024–2025** - recent content on AI + SEO specifically (not just generic SEO)
3. **Signal density** - their content tends to include original data, experiments, or case studies
4. **Platform diversity** - covers YouTube tutorials, LinkedIn frameworks, newsletters, and podcasts
